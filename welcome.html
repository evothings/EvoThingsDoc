Welcome to EvoThings Studio!

Connect your apps to anything, here's how:

  1. Download the mobile Evothings Client from our download page, for Android also from Google Play
  2. Connect your computer and phone by pressing "scan to connect", which works if you're on the same network. Or by entering the Connect URL of your computer (seen in lower right of projects' window).
  3. Client says "Connected"? Good, click Run in the projects' list to run on all connected clients. Updating and saving a file in the project will automatically reload it every time!
  4. Each example has its own tutorial page, under Examples in the documentation.
  5. Drag and drop your own HTML files to the projects' list to add them! If your project has many pages, just add the home page and press Run.
